About


Burrito Burrito was founded in 1971 by Burrito Guy. Burrito guy was a Hippie who lived in the woods, and he really liked burritos.
But Burrito guy was never satified with the town's only burrito shop; Bug Burrito's.
This was probably because the burrito's there had bugs in them. So burrito guy moved out of the woods,
and build a burrito shop in the middle of town. Thats how Burrito Burrito started!

Our mission

Our mission is to bring the best burrito's to everyone who has the chance to go to our store.
We want to make the best burritos we can make!
